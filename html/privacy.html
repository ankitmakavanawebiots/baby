Privacy Policy

BabyG Technology Private Limited (“BabyG App”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy applies to the BabyG Website and BabyG Mobile Application (the “Service”), and BabyG content, such as text, graphics, videos, images, information, and other material (“Content”). It describes how we use, secure, collect and share information of our users as well as individuals whose payment details are used to purchase Content.

1. What Information We Collect

We only collect the information you provide with registration and usage of the Application. You create an account with BabyG Application either through a phone number and password or you can use login with OTP to verify your phone number, or you can connect or sign in to our Service using a third-party service, we will use that third-party service to authenticate you. We may also use, collect or access other information that you have agreed may be provided by that third party, such as your username, name, e-mail address, date of birth, and gender. We collect this information so that it can be used for the purposes explained in this Privacy Policy. To personalize the program for your baby, we ask for information including your name, relationship, child name, gender, and date of birth and if the child is premature, we need the expected date of birth to make adjustments to the program. We also allow you to upload a photo of your child.

When you use the Service, we may also collect and store information about the mobile or other devices you use to access our Service and about how you use them. We may also collect and store information such as IP address, operating system, unique device identifier, mobile network information including the date and time of your usage, the content and pages you view.

We may also collect and store certain information in server logs, including in third-party logs. This may include: (i) details of how you used our Service or Content, (ii) technical information, (iii) device event information such as crashes, system activity, hardware settings, Application launch, the date and time of your request, features used and referral URL, etc.

2. How Do We Use The Information

We use the information we collect to operate and provide you with the BabyG Application Service and Content. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Authenticating your account acess and processing payments;
  • Personalizing your BabyG Application experience and customizing content for your child’s program
  • Allowing you to monitor your child’s progress in BabyG App
  • Customizing and delivering information that may be of interest to you
  • Providing customer service and confirmations about your account
  • Protecting our intellectual property or other rights; and
  • Managing and improving our business, Service, and Content.

You can opt-out of receiving most notifications and emails by selecting the “unsubscribe” link on our emails or from the phone settings, but we reserve the right to email you (i) to confirm transactions, (ii) to address account issues, such as when you make a purchase, or you request a reset password or (iii) as required by law.

We also reserve the right to use or share non-personal information (e.g., un-identified or aggregate data) for any purpose.

We do not sell, share or trade your information with any unrelated third parties without your consent. Your information will not be visible to other users of the Service.

Consent. We may disclose your information when we believe we have your consent to do so, such as when you contact customer service and ask us about your account.

4. Use of cookies

We and our partners use various technologies to collect and store information when you use our Service or Content, and this may include sending one or more cookies or anonymous identifiers to your device. Cookies are small text files placed on the browser of your computer or mobile device when you visit a website or use an application.

We, our business partners and advertisers may use cookies (or clear gifs, web beacons or similar devices, including devices that function in the mobile environment) to collect information from users for purposes such as tracking Service visits and to measure visitor patterns.

This information is not personally identifiable and is used only to improve the Service or Content and enhance your experience. Cookies are not used to retrieve information from your computer or device or to retrieve other information from your computer or device for purposes that are unrelated to the Service or your interaction with the Service. We reserve the right to disclose or utilize any of this information in aggregate form, for any purpose, in our sole judgment. Third-party advertisers that serve ads on the Service may also use their own cookies.

You may stop or restrict the placement of cookies on your device or remove them from your browser by adjusting your web browser preferences. Note that this may cause some features of the Service to work incorrectly. Please note that cookie-based opt-outs are not effective on mobile applications. However, on many mobile devices, application users may opt-out of certain mobile tracking activities via their device settings.
