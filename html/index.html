Why BabyG? The Science Behind

In the first year, baby’s brain doubles in size, and by the third year, 80% of brain development is completed. So unlock their learning potential and they will amaze you!

From the 3rd year, pruning process begins. The brain removes lesser-used neural networks and retains more from the stimulated areas.The key to retain neural networks is early stimulation.

Babies have double the neural networks than that of an adult. Stimulation with activities is a fun way to channelize baby’s energy into learning, as well as boosting the brain connections.

Humans have immense learning capability in the first three years of life and it forms the foundation for lifelong learning. Parenting plays a pivotal role in unlocking a baby’s true potential.
